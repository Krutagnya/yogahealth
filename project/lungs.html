Trikonasana

Stand Tall: Start in a standing position with your feet about 3-4 feet apart. Turn Your Right Foot: Turn your right foot out 90 degrees and your left foot slightly in. Extend Your Arms: Raise your arms parallel to the floor, palms facing down. Reach to the Right: Extend your torso to the right, bending at the hip, and reach your right hand down towards your right ankle. Left Arm Up: Stretch your left arm straight up towards the ceiling, keeping your shoulders aligned. Look Up: Turn your head to look up at your left hand.
